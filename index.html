Two lessons per week (or two students) $190/month.

Siblings enrolled in individual Private Lessons.
Students studying multiple instruments.
Students who would benefit from meeting with their instructor more frequently than once a week.

Master classes Rock classes

Available to existing Rhythm & Keys students $45 a month or $15 per session (per participant).

Master classes allow you to:

  • Get inspired
  • Learn advanced techniques
  • Network with other musicians
  • Pick up performance tips

Or bring your band in for a Rock Class to:

  • Learn new songs
  • Identify your ensemble's strengths and weaknesses
  • Arrange songs you already know in exciting new ways
  • Play your best together
  • Available to existing Rhythm & Keys students at $10/session (per participant).
